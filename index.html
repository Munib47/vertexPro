Frequently Asked Questions

about fridge issues we fix

A fridge that isn't cooling is often caused by dirty condenser coils, a faulty evaporator fan, a failing compressor, or a bad thermostat. Our technicians diagnose the exact cause and restore proper cooling — often the same day.
Water leaks are commonly caused by a clogged defrost drain, a damaged water line, or a faulty water inlet valve. We locate the source and repair it to prevent further damage to your floors.
Frost buildup usually points to a faulty defrost heater, defrost timer, or a worn door gasket letting warm air in. We repair or replace the affected part to stop the frost.
A warm fridge can result from a failed compressor, low refrigerant, blocked vents, or control board issues. Our team pinpoints the problem and gets your appliance cold again fast.
Ice makers stop working due to a frozen water line, faulty water inlet valve, or a broken ice maker assembly. We service ice makers as part of our refrigerator repair for all major brands.
If ice is made but won't dispense, the dispenser motor, auger, or chute may be jammed or faulty. We repair the mechanism so dispensing works smoothly again.
Overflowing ice makers are usually caused by a misaligned unit, a faulty fill valve, or incorrect water pressure. We adjust and repair for consistent, leak-free operation.
Yes — in addition to repairs, our technicians can install and set up washers and most major home appliances safely and correctly. Call us to schedule.
